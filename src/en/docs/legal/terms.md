## What Afinados is

Afinados is a set of tools that produces **comparative geometric estimates** to support carburetor tuning. The tools compute geometry approximations (for example, the fuel-passage area across throttle) from catalog data.

## What Afinados is not

Afinados **does not measure flow** and **does not replace** bench, dyno, or track testing. The results are a reference for comparing setups, not validated jetting advice. **Tune at your own risk.** Eiseron is not liable for engine damage, performance loss, or any harm arising from use of the estimates.
